# Stage Guide

1. discovery (`discover`): ingest and normalize feedback, generate insights.
1. prioritization (`prioritize`): score opportunities and produce roadmap proposal.
1. definition (`define`): produce requirements PRD with goals, requirements, and metrics.
1. delivery_ready (`build_ready`): finalize implementation/test readiness.
1. launch_ready (`release_ready`): finalize rollout/monitoring/rollback.
1. learning (`learn_ready`): capture outcomes and next iteration.
